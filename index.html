About Me

I'm Luca Carlo, born in 1989, husband and father of four wonderful children, two girls and two boys. My passion for computers started when I was six years old and got my first PC running Windows 95. Since then, I've always loved exploring both hardware and software, gradually focusing more on systems, programs, and everything computers can do. Over the past ten years, I've been creating websites and dedicating myself more deeply to both web and software development. I’m constantly learning, driven by curiosity and a genuine love for technology, and that’s something I don’t think will ever change.
